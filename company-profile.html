Company Profile

INBIOS INDIA began its journey in 2004 with Inbios International Inc.USA. After validating and popularising its RK-39 Kalazaar rapid test we went forward with introducing other products in India. Since then we have added a number of products in our portfolio to cater to the exponentially growing demand in the healthcare sector. It's team of well experienced professionals has been successful in establishing fruitful alliances with diagnostic leaders to source their technology and consumables for the benefit of the people of India. It has withstood the test of time and has been successful in penetrating India’s diverse and complicated marketplace.

We have made network all across India, with over 120 distributors who supply our products. We also have more than 50 direct customers (including hospitals, reference labs and research institutes). We are currently a team of 15 members for diagnostics and research

vision & mission

We have planned to expand our business by adding Molecular Diagnostics/ Instruments in our portfolio. We are in a process to start class manufacturing unit at Delhi and the plant size is more than 5000 square feet a rea.

We have plan to manufacture Real Time PCR kits, Serology kit, Rapid and Biochemistry, Elisa etc. We are expanding our sales and technical team to all major city and state.

Hundred Of Clients And Counting

From AIIMS, CMC-Vellore, NIV-Pune, PGI-Chandigarh, SKIMS-JK, Swami Dayanand Hospital, Ludhiana, Manipal Hospital, Dr Lal’s, Metropolis, to Quest and more, we have ‘n’ number of clients in our list and are growing with each passing day.
